Projects

Active

  • [https://asc.llnl.gov/CORAL-benchmarks/ CORAL] and [[Blue Gene/Q]] are my primary projects at the moment.

  • I am involved in both ASCR Leadership Computing Challenge ([https://www.alcf.anl.gov/alcc-program-0 ALCC]) and [http://www.doeleadershipcomputing.org/ INCITE] projects in computer science and chemistry.

  • [https://github.com/jeffhammond/oshmpi OSHMPI] - OpenSHMEM over MPI-3

  • https://trac.mcs.anl.gov/projects/ospri OSPRI

  • [http://wiki.mpich.org/armci-mpi/index.php/Main_Page ARMCI-MPI] - Jim Dinan developed a portable, high-performance implementation of ARMCI using MPI-2 RMA with limited help from me. I am working on the MPI-3 implementation.

  • http://www.nsf.gov/awardsearch/showAward?AWD_ID=1148485&HistoricalAwards=false NSF SI2-SSI: A Linear Algebra Software Infrastructure for Sustained Innovation in Computational Chemistry and other Sciences.

  • http://ctop.cs.utah.edu/x-tune/ DOE X-Stack: X-Tune

  • [[NWChem]] - I developed the coupled-cluster response property capability, among other features, during graduate school. Static partitioning (load-balancing), threading, vectorization and accelerator integrations for NWChem are currently under investigation.

  • [[MADNESS]] - [http://iacs.stonybrook.edu/people/faculty/robert-j-harrison Robert Harrison] and I ported MADNESS to Blue Gene/P. [http://www.alcf.anl.gov/staff-directory/alvaro-vazquez-mayagoitia Alvaro Vazquez-Mayagoitia], [https://www.alcf.anl.gov/staff-directory/nichols-romero Nick Romero], and others are involved in the Blue Gene/Q effort, which includes both performance improvements and new scientific functionality.

  • [http://www.emsl.pnl.gov/docs/global/ Global Arrays] - I contributed features and tried to maintain the Blue Gene/P port until converting wholly to the use of [http://wiki.mpich.org/armci-mpi/index.php/Main_Page ARMCI-MPI]. Described [http://www.cscs.ch/fileadmin/SOS15_presentations/Robert_Harrison.pdf here], [http://exascaleresearch.labworks.org/uploads/dataforms/C_OPH_ORNL_Chemistry_110304.pdf here], and [http://exascaleresearch.labworks.org/uploads/dataforms/C_PRES_ORNL_Chemistry_110303.pdf here].
